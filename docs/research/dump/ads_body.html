FAQs

Who can advertise on Peerlist?

Any company or startup looking to reach tech professionals, designers, developers and indie hackers can run ads on Peerlist.

What ad placements are available?

We currently offer 4 placements: Side Panel Widget, Scroll Banner, Jobs List Banner, Launchpad Feed. Each offers unique visibility and engagement opportunities.

How long is an ad slot?

An ad slot is 1 week long.

Can I book multiple slots at the same time?

Absolutely. You can book multiple slots or placements to maximize your reach.

Can I edit my ad after it has gone live?

Yes, absolutely. Send your changes to us on ads@peerlist.io.

How do I track performance?

You will receive a weekly report of your ad performance via email.

Can you reject my ad?

Yes. Once you submit your ad, it will be under review. Full refund in case we decide not to proceed with your request.
